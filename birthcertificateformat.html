Birth Certificate Website Guide

How to Apply for a Birth Certificate Online

  1. Open the official Birth Registration Portal.
  2. Create a new account or log in using your credentials.
  3. Select "Birth Registration" or "Apply for Birth Certificate".
  4. Fill in all required details:
    • Child's Name
    • Date of Birth
    • Place of Birth
    • Father's Name
    • Mother's Name
    • Address
  5. Upload the required documents (if asked).
  6. Verify all details carefully.
  7. Click the Submit button.
  8. Save your Application Number for future reference.
  9. You can later check the application status or download the certificate if available.
Note: Keep your registration number and supporting documents safe. They may be required for verification or downloading the certificate later.
